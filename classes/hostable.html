endGameIntents

endGameIntents: Map<string, () => any>
  • Defined in node_modules/@skeldjs/core/dist/lib/Hostable.d.ts:160

Information for controlling when the game shoudl end, this dictionary is checked every fixed update to look for reasons to end a game.

See RoomEndGameIntentEvent to cancel or listen for end game intent criteria being fulfilled. Or see Hostable.registerEndGameIntent to register your own.

See RoomGameEndEvent to listen for a game actually ending.
